About TNDCE

The Tamil Nadu Directorate of Collegiate Education (TNDCE) is responsible for the administration and development of government and aided colleges in Tamil Nadu. It aims to provide quality higher education to students across the state.

Colleges

  • Government Arts College, Chennai
  • Government College of Engineering, Tirunelveli
  • Queen Mary's College, Chennai
  • PSG College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore
  • Madras Christian College, Chennai

Courses

  • Bachelor of Arts (BA)
  • Bachelor of Science (BSc)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (BCom)
  • Bachelor of Engineering (BE)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Admissions

Admissions to undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Tamil Nadu are conducted through a centralized online process. Students can apply through the official TNDCE portal.

Scholarships

The Tamil Nadu government offers various scholarships for meritorious and economically disadvantaged students. Apply online through the TNDCE portal.

  • Merit Scholarship
  • BC/MBC Scholarship
  • SC/ST Scholarship
