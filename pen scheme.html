About Pudhumai Penn Scheme

The Pudhumai Penn Scheme is an initiative by the Tamil Nadu Government to provide financial assistance to girl students pursuing higher education. The scheme aims to empower girls and encourage them to continue their education.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Girl students studying in government or government-aided colleges.
  • Students must be residents of Tamil Nadu.
  • Family income should not exceed â‚¹2.5 lakh per annum.
  • Applicable for undergraduate and postgraduate courses.

Apply Online

Eligible students can apply for the Pudhumai Penn Scheme online. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Visit the official Pudhumai Penn Scheme portal.
  2. Register with your details.
  3. Fill in the application form.
  4. Upload necessary documents (income certificate, mark sheets, etc.).
  5. Submit the application.
