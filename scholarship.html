About SSP

The Tamil Nadu Scholarship Scheme Portal (SSP) is a platform for students to apply for various scholarships offered by the Tamil Nadu government. The portal aims to provide financial assistance to deserving students for their education.

Scholarships

  • Merit Scholarship: For students with outstanding academic performance.
  • BC/MBC Scholarship: For students belonging to Backward Classes and Most Backward Classes.
  • SC/ST Scholarship: For students belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
  • Minority Scholarship: For students from minority communities.
  • Disabled Scholarship: For students with disabilities.

Apply Online

Students can apply for scholarships online through the SSP portal. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Register on the SSP portal.
  2. Fill in the required details.
  3. Upload necessary documents.
  4. Submit the application.
