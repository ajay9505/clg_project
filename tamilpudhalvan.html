Tamil Nadu Pudhu Vaazhvu Scheme

The Tamil Nadu Pudhu Vaazhvu Scheme (தமிழ்நாடு புதுவாழ்வுத் திட்டம்) is a welfare initiative by the Government of Tamil Nadu aimed at empowering rural communities, particularly women, by promoting self-help groups (SHGs) and improving livelihoods.

Key Features of the Scheme

  • Target Group: Rural women, especially those below the poverty line (BPL).
  • Components: Formation of SHGs, financial assistance, skill development, and infrastructure development.
  • Implementation: Managed by the Tamil Nadu Corporation for Development of Women (TNCDW).
  • Funding: Supported by the World Bank and the Government of Tamil Nadu.

How to Apply

To apply for the Tamil Nadu Pudhu Vaazhvu Scheme, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official website of TNCDW.
  2. Look for the Pudhu Vaazhvu Scheme section.
  3. Fill out the application form and submit it with the required documents.
