About Our College

History of our College Karur is one of the coastal districts of Kadt Cauvery river, which is the heart of this district. Majority of the people living here are very backward in terms of socio-economic status. They used to send their children to the nearby towns of Karur or Tiruchirappalli for higher education. They wanted a government college to be set up in Kuluthalai circle so that their children could get higher education. Government Arts College has now come into existence to fulfill their desire. the Accepting the request of the member of Kulithalai Legislative Assembly, during the speech on higher education grant request held in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 4.5.2007, the Hon'ble Minister of Higher Education said that a Government Arts College will be established at Kulithalai in the academic year 2007-2008 by Government Order (Status) No. 270, Higher Education (G1) Department, dated 3.8.2007. announced. It was decided on 5.5.2007 that 22.18 acres of land at Ayyarmalai near Kulithalai would be selected for the new college (Order No. 33 Higher Education (G1) Department, dated 25.9.2007) and permanent buildings would be constructed. On 3.9.2007 the Government of Tamil Nadu issued an order naming the college as Dr. Kalainar Government Art College (Legislature Golden Festival).
