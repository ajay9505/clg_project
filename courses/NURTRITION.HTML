M.S.C Nurtrition & Dietetics course details


MSc Nutrition and Dietetics is a 2 Year postgraduate course which specially deals with the principles of the Nutritional values in Food and their impacts on the Human Body.


What is the scope of MSc in Nutrition and Dietetics?


The MSc Nutrition and Dietetics course graduates can work in both the private and public sectors in roles like Dietitian, Nutritionist, Public Health Dietitian, Food Service Director, Food Scientist, Nutrition Consultant, Nutrition Education, Clinical Dietitian,


Who is eligible for MSc dietitian?


Eligibility for M.Sc Food, Nutrition and Dietetics: The minimum qualification for the eligibility for M.Sc in Food, Nutrition and Dietetics is a minimum 50% in Bachelor's degree in a related field, such as Food Science, Nutrition, Dietetics, Home Science, or a relevant discipline.


What is MSc Dietetic?


M. Sc (Nutrition and Dietetics) is a 2-year program with four semesters and having specialization in three branches viz, Clinical Nutrition, Public Health Nutrition, Food Science and Nutrition. Students will choose this specialization in third semester of the program.
